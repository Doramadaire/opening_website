Chrystèle Lerisse was born in Le Mans in 1960. She uses, in an immutable way silver halide photography and black and white in her work and destroys her negatives after having made the prints. Her photographs have been shown in France, in Europe and in Asia in gallerys, museums, art centers and numerous art fairs : Paris Photo, Art Paris, Aipad New York, Art Bâle, Art Brussels, Montrouge Salon.
At her invitation Opening selected from her photography archives thirty images from different time periods and series. Landscapes, bodies, volumes make up this photographic sequence where the photographic reality is above all one of space and light.
