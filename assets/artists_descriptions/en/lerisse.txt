Chrystèle Lerisse was born in Mans in 1960. She uses, in an immutable way  silver halide photography and black and white in her work and destroys her negatives after having made the prints.  Her photographs have been shown in France, in Europe and in Asia in gallerys, museums, art centers and numerous  art fairs. Paris Photo, Art Paris, Aipad New York, Art Bâle, Art Brussels, Montrouge Salon.
