Opening book presents a selection of the photographic mise-en-scène from two series : The Grands Rangements et les Ustensiles (Storage/tidying and utensils). "It sparks off that unending oscillation of interpretation that hesitates between nobility and vulgarity, undermining the spectators confidence in his approach to these images, enhanced and kept at a distance with a refined and complex presentation." (Jean-Christian Fleury)
