The series “ Nevermind” is a turning point in André Mérian's work, a new approach to capturing reality. “This ensemble of images amplifies the troubling game that looking entails in the process of identifying with a photographic work. That which is there announces that which is evident. These images state something else even more essential, of an hidden order, almost invisible which weaves between things. “Nevermind” is above all a savant game of revelations, revelations of the fortuitous arrangements of things (...)” A Mérian
