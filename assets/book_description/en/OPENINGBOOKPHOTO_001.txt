First title in a new collection dedicated to photography, openingbook photo 001 presents twelve photographs by Monique Deregibus, extracts from different series undertaken in Cyprus, Sicily and the south of France. The places, the photographed sites all carry the imprint of their more or less recent and often violent history.  The images here are processed in semi-darkness, in which detail abounds. The book constitutes a privileged form of reproduction of Monique Deregibus's photos because it is " open and it is up to the reader themselves to travel and construct their own story inside the proffered offering. "
