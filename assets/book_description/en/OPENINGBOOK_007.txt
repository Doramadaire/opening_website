Opening presents twelve monotypes from the serie "Nuages"*. Monotype is a process of contact or manual impression without engraving. Joséphine de Saint Seine writes or draws directly on the back of the sheet of paper (back to front therefore) whilst inking, which creates a unique item. The sheet of paper is lifted from the inked plate, the image appears the right way round, with its traces and vibrations, its uncontrolled line and its great lightness marrying the subject : the cloud.  *"Clouds"
