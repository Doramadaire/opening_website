
The photographs were selected from Geirmundur Klein's recent works and put to a poem by Michel Houellebecq.

Geirmundur photographs mostly in daily life without any specific subject in mind, sometimes referring to it as keeping a visual diary. 

The dutch photographer Marijn de Jong ones wrote of his work: "...the photographs show how he curiously observes the world around him. I see a solitary, but honest and loving glance that is understanding to the world and people wrestling within it..."
